Want to know more about Honey Boom?

Our apiary was founded by Jonathan Rodfil back in 1982. He bought one evidence and was developing his apiary on his farm in Texas. Five years later he already had a huge apiary. Today, Jonathan's relatives continue his family business and sell the sweetest honey in all of Texas.
